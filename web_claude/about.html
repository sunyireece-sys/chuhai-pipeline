— About —

A single source,
built for brands.

Redvia is the international ingredient brand of Bairuiyuan Goji Co. Ltd. — a 20-year-old certified organic plantation in Ningxia, supplying formulators, distributors and brand owners worldwide.

Bairuiyuan has farmed goji in Ningxia since 2003. Two decades of cultivation, processing, and quality work on the same 12,000-mu plantation. The same berries become every form in the portfolio — whole, puree, powder, leaf tea, seed oil.

Redvia is the brand we use to serve formulators, distributors, and brand owners outside China. One operation, one source — without the supply-chain hops that obscure origin and spec.

— Ningxia · Source —

Bairuiyuan Goji Co. Ltd.

The agricultural and processing entity. 12,000-mu organic plantation, on-site processing lines for berries, puree, powder, and oil. Certified by Kiwa BCS for EU, USDA NOP, and JAS Organic. BRCGS Grade B. CNAS-accredited in-house laboratory.

This is where everything is grown, processed, packed, and tested before it leaves China.

— International Supply —

Redvia, the international brand.

Redvia serves international clients directly — formulators in North America and Europe, distributors in the Middle East, Russia, and Southeast Asia, and brand owners building goji-forward wellness products.
