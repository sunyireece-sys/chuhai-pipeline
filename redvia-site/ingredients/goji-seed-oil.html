Goji Seed Oil

Premium botanical lipid for beauty nutrition and skin wellness.

A high-value botanical oil extracted from goji seeds via supercritical CO₂ extraction. Naturally rich in linoleic acid (65.24–66.26%), tocopherols, phytosterols, polyphenols, and carotenoids. Well suited for premium wellness, beauty nutrition, skincare serums, and cosmetic crossover products.

Botanical seed oil for beauty nutrition and skincare systems.
